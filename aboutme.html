What should I do to start a business?

We can look for some big companion, what they exactly doing when they want open some store or show room, when they going to make a product, and any steps that we can apply in our business. Some great quotation from Mohammad Ali "If your dreams don't scare you, they aren't big enough", so lets made brand new days, make some big dreams, thats the first point. And the second point is, determination. It is a hard conditions, and the hardest step is to decide an idea. But, if we know how, then nothing to worry at all. The fun way to determine ideas is with Market Research. Third point is write down all your needs for that business into a paper, never keep your idea in your head, you will lose it. Breaking your mind as small part as you can, that called Mind Mapping. All the big company do that, And even, there are some special companies that work in that part, they are rented by big companies. They have some group of experts, sit together, they exchange ideas, it's called Brain Storming, then they start drawing it, thats the fourth point. Meanwhile, you can exchange ideas with friends or family, then you write down good ideas that you get. After this, you will feel the ease of starting a business.
