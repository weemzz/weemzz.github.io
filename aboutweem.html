Simple Advice
and Suggestion

This is a suggestion that I got from my experience. you should start learning programming from HTML and CSS, because you will get a very useful base from here, but if you want programming for something else it can be done with an application or other learning programs. By learning how to create a website with HTML and CSS you will have very extensive knowledge, I will explain a few branches of the lesson you will get by learning HTML and CSS.

1.

You will be able to create static sites like the one you are visiting now. And even you can create a dynamic website, because HTML and CSS are the basis of a website building. Whether it's e-commerce, personal, blog, landing page, or other types of sites.

2.

You will find out the different types of sites, and their uses. And even you can experiment, with programming, imagination has no limits. Looking for inspirations, and start make an actions. See how to make a Website for Beginner.

3.

You will be able to use design software, such as Corel or Photoshop, and even others to create logos, compress photos to be small in size without changing the quality, or other parts of the site design. The design of a website is important, because this user interface will interact with users. You can find great inspiration from Smashing Magazine, CSS heaven, CSS garden and more, just google it.

4.

Psychology, yes you will get basic psychological knowledge from humans, this is not nonsense. From the selection of colors that you must match with the theme, such as whether a site sells baby equipment with a black site? or RPG game site, with a pink base with Teddy doll decorations?

5.

Layout that also relates to Psychology, how one site visitor interacts. Imagine when you visit a site, your eyes will spontaneously see the biggest image of that site, so try to draw a picture of what this site is, so when accessing, visitors immediately get information about this site, without having to click or move the cursor. See a full description of the user's Psychology on the "Psychology of the Website" page.

6.

You will have a more specific knowledge of the world, if you create a Blog about your opinion about war in the Middle East, you have to browse or read to collect data first, from there you will get a lesson, right? Or you create an e-commerce site, sell automotive equipment, from the questions that visitors ask will be info for you, what is the most requested in the automotive world today, and that can be a marketing consideration for you, make a big banner on the start page sites that describe you providing a product that is being requested a lot.

How to start
Business

We can look for some big companion, what they exactly doing when they want open some store or show room, when they going to make a product, nd any steps that we can apply in our business. Some great quotation from Mohammad Ali "If your dreams don't scare you, they aren't big enough", so lets made your brand new days, take a head, and make my day, thats the first point.
And the second point is, determination. It is a hard conditions, and the hardest step is to decide an idea. But, if we know how, then nothing to worry at all. The fun way to determine ideas is with Market Research, for the online market you can use Google Trends or Google keywords Tool, here some tutorial to do that. Third point is write down all your needs for that business into a paper, never keep your idea in your head, you will lose it. Breaking your mind as small part as you can, that called Mind Mapping.
All the big company do that, And even, there are some special companies that work in that part, they are rented by big companies. They have some group of experts, sit together, they exchange ideas, it's called Brain Storming, then they start drawing it, thats the fourth point. Meanwhile, you can exchange ideas with friends or family, then you write down good ideas that you get. After this, you will feel the ease of starting a business.
